KIRA is a financial hub connecting decentralized Web3 applications

As an integral part of the Web3 stack KIRA enables value settlement between dApps and largest interconnected networks in the cryptocurrency ecosystem.

Thanks to uncapped value at stake originating from both real world and interchain space KIRA provides secure Layer 1 platform for deploying decentralized applications and issuing digital assets.

01 Multi-Bonded Proof of Stake (MBPoS) The first consensus enabling scaling of both throughput and security of the network, as well as natively supporting liquid staking of any fungible and non fungible tokens. MBPoS makes it possible to secure dApps with the value of real assets at stake and settle high value transfers originating from the cross-chain ecosystem. 02 Interchain Exchange Protocol (IXP) The first sharded AMM supporting cross-chain communication protocols to enable permissionless listing and trading of staking derivatives without missing out on the block and fee rewards. IXP will be the first dApp on the KIRA to demonstrate full scaling capabilities of the network. 03 Initial Validator Offering (IVO) The first decentralized launchpad enabling crowdfunding without spending assets, locking value or giving out custody over your tokens to any third parties. IVO will further propel dApp development on KIRA Network and beyond.
